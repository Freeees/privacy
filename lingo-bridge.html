Lingo Bridge – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Lingo Bridge ("the App") is a local-only shopping list application. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

2. Information We Collect

Lingo Bridge does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account creation
  • No login system
  • No cloud storage
  • No analytics
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No tracking technologies

We do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics
  • Shopping list content

3. Local Data Storage

All data created within the App (including shopping lists and settings) is stored locally on your device. Data is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible by the developer.

When you uninstall the App, all associated data is permanently deleted by the operating system.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any information with third parties. Since we do not collect data, there is nothing to share.

5. Children's Privacy

Lingo Bridge does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Because no personal data is collected, the App complies with applicable child privacy regulations.

6. GDPR (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • No personal data is processed
  • No personal data is stored
  • No profiling occurs
  • No cross-border data transfer occurs

7. Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored externally, there are no remote security risks related to personal data collection. All data remains solely on the user's device.

8. Third-Party Services

Lingo Bridge does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if App functionality changes in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
